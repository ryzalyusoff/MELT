How to Add & Set Exam ?

  • Step 1: On the Main page, click on the "EXAM" button



  • Step 2: On the left panel, click on the "Add a Exam" button



  • Step 3: Fill in the instruction for the Exam, and click "OK" button



  • Step 4: Newly created exam will appear on the left panel. Now select the exam and click the "Edit button"



  • Step 5: An Exam shall has at least one section. To add section, click on the "Add a section" button



  • Step 6: Set the section name, answering time and the number of subsections that this exam should have. Then, click "OK" button



  • Step 7: Add questions to by clicking the Add question button.



  • Step 8: Select the question you want to add, then click "Add Question" button



  • Step 9: After you have added all the questions, click "Create Section" button



  • Step 10: Now you should see a new Section appeared on the left panel. You can click "Edit" button to edit the section or click "Delete" button to delete it



  • Step 11: To publish/activate the exam, click "Back to Exams" button. Select the exam, and click "Activate" button
